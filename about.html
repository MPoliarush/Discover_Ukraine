Brief facts

Information snippets

Location

As a sovereign state, Ukraine was formed in 1991. Ukraine is bordered by 7 countries and has access to the Black Sea and the Sea of Azov.

Symbol

Ukraine's flag has 2 horizontal lines. Blue colour denotes the sky, while yellow means the grain growing in the huge wheat fields beneath the blue sky.

Language

Ukrainian is the single official language in country. That language is considered to be one of the most beautiful languages in the world, after French and Italian. It is very melodic and easy on the ear.

Cuisine

Borsch and pampushky with garlic are "visiting card" of Ukraine. Borsch is a soup with vegetables such as carrot, cabbage, beet, potato, onion and tomatos.
